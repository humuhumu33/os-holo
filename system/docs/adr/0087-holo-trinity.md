**Context.** Five ADRs built the parts of a self-improving generative system — a fleet of tiny specialists (0084), content-addressed compute (0085), a renderer (0085), perception (0086), a self-improvement loop (0086), and the orchestrator binding (0085). And the triality exploration (the orchestrator↔interpreter synthesis) gave the *shape*: the relationship is not a dyad but a **triad** — creating (immanent), existing (transcendent), perceiving (omniscient) — reconciled by content-addressing as the shared ground where observer meets observed. What was missing is the move from *parts* to *one experience*: a way to deliver all of it across the whole OS and its native apps as a single, simple thing, rather than six modules each app must assemble. The design constraint is the hardest one: **abstract all the complexity, deliver simplicity** — and keep it 100% serverless, very low latency, continuously self-improving.

**Decision.** Ship the trinity as one composition root every app inherits, with the metaphysics as the architecture:

1. **CREATE (immanent) — sovereign, ungated, fast.** `create()` routes a generative/compute act through the fabric (the interpreter's faculty). It is the interpreter's sovereign act, so it is NOT conscience-gated and not slowed — creation is free. Every output SEALS to a κ.
2. **EXIST (transcendent) — automatic, shared.** The κ-substrate grounds every output: content-addressed, memoized (cross-app O(1)), receipted. This is the commons neither faculty owns — where observer and observed meet. The app does nothing for it; it is the medium.
3. **PERCEIVE (omniscient) — automatic, governed.** Perception (0086) registers every object's code+visual faces and the self-improvement loop heals drift, optimizes latency, and learns — but this is the *orchestrator's* faculty, so it is conscience-GOVERNED: it edits only what EXISTS, never the interpreter's live act. **The ethic is the architecture:** creation is sovereign; improvement is governed.

The **precedence** is enforced structurally: each `create` seals to a κ *before* `perceive` can touch it — create-then-edit, never both at once (the exploration's central result). Streaming is the same loop at micro-granularity (each fragment sealed before it is perceived), so "real-time" never violates the ordering.

**Abstracting the complexity.** (a) The shell installs ONE OS-wide trinity (one scene, one fabric, one loop) — not one per app. (b) Apps **conform, they don't wire**: using the OS render/compute seams auto-feeds the trinity (the renderer's `onFinal` registers the visual face, the fabric the code face). (c) The whole developer surface is `mount({ target })` once and `create(intent)` per act — exist and perceive are automatic. The six modules become invisible behind two calls. The user's surface is *nothing* — the loop is below the waterline; they just see things appear and improve.

**Continuously self-improving — and still simple.** A cheap ambient in-tab ticker runs the loop; the scene-κ makes an unchanged pass nearly free, so it can run often at no cost. It heals across ALL apps in one OS-wide scene, governed by the conscience, and appends a trace per improvement (the corpus the orchestrator evolves on, 0081). The heal re-creates from CURRENT state (a `params` thunk re-read at heal time, so any state the act reads is in the memo key and the re-derivation is correct) — verified live: drift under a new theme healed to the new theme, not the old.

**Honest costs.** Coverage is as wide as conformance: an app that bypasses the OS seams is invisible to the trinity until it `mount`s + `create`s (one-time wiring per app, deferred for the existing apps). `create` is task-agnostic — an app passes a `provider` or a mux task; "create" is just the default label, not a registered specialist. The ambient ticker is opt-in (the shell starts it) and its cadence is a real knob. Output quality is bounded by the on-device specialists (0084). And the trinity inherits every honest cost of its parts (the raster-edge VLM, the in-mem index, the bounded specialist catalog).

**Composition.** The unification of 0084·0085·0086 under the orchestrator (0081), grounded in the κ-substrate (0052/0024/0025) and the conscience (0033), shaped by the triality synthesis. Mints nothing — the trinity is the three modes the system already had, composed. Pending: the shell install + ambient ticker, app conformance, and a `#holo-trinity` witness row + os-closure re-lock.
